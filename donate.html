Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to Nandini Dharmartha Gau Seva Trust?▼
You can donate via UPI (QR919953167250-0104@unionbankofindia), WhatsApp Pay, bank transfer, or cheque. After donating, please send a screenshot to +91 9719529986 on WhatsApp so we can send your receipt.
Is my donation tax exempt?▼
Yes! All donations to Nandini Dharmartha Gau Seva Trust are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Please provide your PAN number at the time of donation to receive your 80G certificate.
How will I receive my donation receipt?▼
After your donation is confirmed, we will send your receipt via WhatsApp or Email within 2–3 working days. Please share correct contact details when donating.
How will my donation be used?▼
Your donation goes directly towards feeding, rescuing, and providing medical care to cows. We maintain full transparency and share monthly updates with our donors on WhatsApp.
Can I donate in someone's memory or honour?▼
Yes! Visit our Support Us page and select "Donate on Special Occasion." You can donate in memory or honour of a loved one and receive a beautiful e-certificate.
Is my personal information safe?▼
Absolutely. We take data privacy very seriously. Your personal information will never be shared with any third party, and we do not store any sensitive payment information.
