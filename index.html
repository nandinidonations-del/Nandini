Who We Are
❧

Our Sacred Mission

We believe every cow deserves a life free from suffering. Since 2024, Nandini Dharmartha Gau Seva Trust has been at the forefront of cow rescue, care, and rehabilitation.

🤲

Vriddhashram

We provide food, clothing, and essential daily support to the most vulnerable and underprivileged families in our community — because every soul deserves dignity.

💊

Medical Care

Qualified veterinarians and caregivers provide complete medical treatment, ensuring every rescued cow recovers fully and comfortably.

🏠

Safe Shelter

Our gaushala provides a permanent, loving home with nutritious food, clean water, and daily care for every resident cow.

📣

Awareness

We educate communities about cow welfare, inspiring thousands to become advocates for Gau Raksha across our region.

What We Do
❧

Our Work — Beyond Gau Seva

Nandini Dharmartha Gau Seva Trust works across five causes — from protecting cows to empowering children education, caring for elders, sheltering the homeless, and healing our environment.

📚

Unpriviledged Child Education

Free school supplies, books & uniforms for children from Unprivileged families who cannot afford basic education material.
👴

Old Age Sahayata

Food, medicines, clothing & companionship for elderly people living alone or abandoned without family support.
🐦

Gauriya Seva

We protect sparrows by providing food, clean water and safe nesting spaces — bringing गौरैया back to our lives.
🌿

Environment

Road & river cleaning drives, tree plantation, and anti-pollution awareness campaigns in our communities.
🐄

Gau Seva

Our core mission — 24/7 rescue, medical care & permanent shelter for abandoned and injured cows at our gaushala.
