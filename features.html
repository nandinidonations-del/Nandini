Rescued

Kamdhenu's Story

Kamdhenu was found injured after an accident, with wounds on three of her legs. Our team rescued her and provided three months of intensive medical care. Today she is completely healthy and thriving! 🐄

📅 January 2026✅ Recovered

Abandoned

Nandini's New Life

Nandini was found abandoned alone in a field — severely weak and starving. We brought her to the gaushala and nursed her back to health. She now eats happily from our volunteers' hands and is loved by all! 😄

📅 February 2026✅ Happy
🐄
old cow

serving an old cow

An old cow once lived near our village, weak and tired after years of hard life. Many people ignored her, but we decided to take care of her. Every day, we gave her fresh food, clean water, and a safe place to rest. Slowly, she became healthier and her eyes began to shine again. Taking care of her taught us kindness, compassion, and the importance of helping those who cannot help themselves.

Medical Care

Gauri's Victory

Gauri was suffering from a serious skin infection when she arrived. Our vet, Dr. Ramesh, treated her continuously for six weeks. Her remarkable recovery moved everyone at the gaushala to tears — a true victory! 💪

📅 December 2025✅ Cured

Rescued

Lakshmi — A New Dawn

Lakshmi was rescued from an illegal slaughterhouse and arrived frightened and deeply traumatised. Our caregivers showered her with patience and love. Today she is the most calm and gentle cow in our gaushala. ❤️

📅 November 2025✅ Trusts Us

Elderly

Shyama — A Peaceful Old Age

Shyama is an 18-year-old cow who was abandoned by her owner. She has become the "grandmother" of our gaushala — always keeping a watchful, caring eye over all the younger calves! 🥰

📅 October 2025🏡 Forever Home
